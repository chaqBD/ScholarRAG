How It Works

Four components, one pipeline — from raw PDFs to cited answers

PDF Extraction

pdfminer pulls the abstract, introduction and conclusion from every paper — highest-signal sections only.

Keyword Retrieval

Stopwords stripped, keywords scored against every document. Top 4 papers selected per query — no embeddings needed.

Claude Synthesis

Extracted paper text becomes Claude’s context. Answers are grounded in those documents — zero hallucination.

Cited Answer

Every claim traces back to a specific paper (“According to RL-7…”) so you can verify the exact source.
